Welcome to the QMRA Tool for Water Reuse

This tool is designed to support Quantitative Microbial Risk Assessment (QMRA) for human health in the context of water reuse. Whether you're evaluating the safety of treated wastewater or other reclaimed water sources, this tool helps you assess potential health risks associated with microbial contaminants. This tool is completely free to use.
